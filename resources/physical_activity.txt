:Regular physical activity supports heart health, mental wellbeing, weight management, and overall fitness. Users often ask how much movement they need, step goals, or simple ways to stay active during the day.

RECOMMENDATIONS:
- Adults: At least 150 minutes of moderate activity per week or 75 minutes of vigorous activity.
- Daily step goal: 7,000–10,000 steps.
- Include strength training 2–3 times per week.
- Take short movement breaks during long periods of sitting.
- Stretch after exercise to improve flexibility and prevent injury.

COMMON PROBLEMS:
- Sedentary lifestyle with long sitting periods.
- Low motivation to exercise.
- Fatigue or discomfort after starting new routines.
- Inconsistent step tracking or goals.
