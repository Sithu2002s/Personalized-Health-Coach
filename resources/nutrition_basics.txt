Balanced nutrition provides energy, supports immunity, maintains healthy weight, and promotes overall wellbeing. Users often ask about healthy meal composition, nutrient intake, and simple diet tips.

RECOMMENDATIONS:
- Eat a variety of fruits and vegetables daily.
- Choose whole grains over refined grains.
- Include lean proteins such as fish, poultry, legumes, and nuts.
- Consume healthy fats like olive oil, avocado, and nuts.
- Limit added sugars, sugary drinks, and ultra-processed foods.
- Drink water regularly to support digestion and metabolism.
- Maintain consistent meal timing to stabilize energy levels.

COMMON PROBLEMS:
- Skipping meals or irregular eating patterns.
- Relying heavily on processed or fast foods.
- Low intake of fruits and vegetables.
- Excess sugar consumption.
- Poor hydration affecting digestion and energy.
