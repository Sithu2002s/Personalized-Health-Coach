Healthy habits support overall physical, mental, and emotional wellbeing. They include sleep, hydration, nutrition, movement, stress management, and consistent daily routines. Users often search for simple, actionable lifestyle tips.

RECOMMENDATIONS:
- Sleep 7–9 hours per night with a consistent schedule.
- Drink 2–3 liters of water per day, adjusted for activity and climate.
- Include at least 7000–10,000 steps daily.
- Eat balanced meals including fruits, vegetables, whole grains, and lean proteins.
- Limit processed foods and added sugars.
- Take short movement or relaxation breaks during work.
- Maintain a consistent morning and evening routine.

COMMON PROBLEMS:
- Skipping meals or irregular eating patterns.
- Low daily activity due to sedentary lifestyle.
- Poor sleep habits affecting energy and focus.
- Difficulty maintaining hydration.
- High stress leading to unhealthy coping behaviors.
