Regular exercise improves energy levels, cardiovascular health, strength, and mood. People often search for simple routines, beginner workouts, home exercises, or step-based activity plans.

RECOMMENDATIONS:
- Beginner: 10–15 minutes brisk walking, 5 days a week.
- Intermediate: 30 minutes walking or cycling, 4–5 days a week.
- Advanced: 40–60 minutes moderate–vigorous exercise, 4–6 days a week.
- Include strength training 2–3 times weekly.
- Warm up for 5 minutes and cool down after finishing.
- Gradually increase intensity to avoid injury.

COMMON PROBLEMS:
- Inconsistency due to time limits.
- Low motivation.
- Fatigue after long sitting hours.
- Difficulty starting workouts as a beginner.
